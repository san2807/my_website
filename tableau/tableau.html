Bar Charts

* visually represent the data in a way that makes comparison of value across different categories easy to compare. [sanb1]
* length is primary means by which u will visualize data. [sanb1]
* may incorporate color, size, stacking & order to communicate additional attributes & values [sanb1]

Line Charts
* connect the releated marks in a visualization to show movement or relationship between connected marks.
* position of marks & the line that connect them r primary means of communicating data.
* u can use size & color to visually communicate additional information.
* most common type of line chart is time-series.
        * time-series show movement of values over time.
