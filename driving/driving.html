Driving

1. If u r first at red light, always check right n left before u move
2. At T intersection, stop and always check if any car is coming



Kerb Side Parking

1. left signal (before 5 seconds)
2. before changing sterring direction u shld check ur mirrors and head check ur left side blind spot
3. park the car
4. tyre should not touch kerb
5. stop
6. turn off indicator
7. apply hand brake
8. select park or first gear
9. turn off the engine
10. remove the key
11. remove ur hands & feet from all controls (as u would like to leave)
12. To restart, right signal (before 5 seconds) do this as first thing so that by the time u do other activities 5 seconds will be over.
13. check ur mirrors and head check ur right blind spot

Reverse Parallel Parking:

1. signal left
2. take ur car mirror till other car steering
3. rotate anti-clockwise three half (first straight steering then rotate it three half times)
4. check right blind spot
5. see back
6. start reversing
7. see the camera,once red line in the camera touch kerb
8. rotate sterring all the way to the clockwise direction.
9. apply hand brake
10. select park or first gear
11. right signal (for 5 seconds)
12. select R
13. remove hand brake
14. check right blind spot
15. stop and wait if any car is comming in side mirror
16. move
