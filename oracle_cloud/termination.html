Terminations





Ending Employment
when terminate w/r:
    1. any assignment associated with w/r r ended automatically
    2. status of w/r & associated assignments r changed to inactive on the day following the termination date.
    3. period of service both LE & enterprise ends on the termination date. The person becomes ex-employee for that LE
    4. person's user access & roles r revoked by default after termination date.



Reverse Terminations




Termination of person will not have any update on per_all_people_f.
It only update per_all_assignments_m and per_periods_of_service

A new record added in per_all_assignments_m
Columns updated in per_all_assignments_m on termination:
assignment_status_type_id            e.g. 3
assignment_status_type                 e.g. INACTIVE
action_code                                     e.g. RESIGNATION
reason_code                                     e.g. RESIGN_PERSONAL
