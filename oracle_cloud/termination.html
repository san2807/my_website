Ending Employment
when terminate w/r:
    1. any assignment associated with w/r r ended automatically
    2. status of w/r & associated assignments r changed to inactive on the day following the termination date.
    3. period of service both LE & enterprise ends on the termination date. The person becomes ex-employee for that LE
    4. person's user access & roles r revoked by default after termination date.
